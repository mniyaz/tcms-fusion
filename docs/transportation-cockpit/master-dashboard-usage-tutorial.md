# Master Dashboard Usage Tutorial

The **Master Dashboard** is where you view and manage your bookings. This guide shows you how to filter what you see, customize the table, and perform common actions.

## Where to find it

Open **Master Dashboard** from the main navigation. The data refreshes automatically every 5 minutes.

---

## Filter by Status

Use the coloured buttons to show only bookings in a certain stage:

| Button | What it shows |
|--------|----------------|
| **All** | Everything |
| **Booked** | Bookings waiting to be assigned |
| **Assigned** | Bookings with truck and driver assigned |
| **YTD** | Yet to deliver |
| **Picked** | Pickup done |
| **Delivered** | Delivery done |

Click a button to filter. Click **All** to show everything again.

---

## My Filters (Customer & User)

Use **My Filters** to always see only certain customers or your own bookings. These filters stay when you save them and apply every time you open the dashboard.

### Opening My Filters

Click **My Filters** in the filter bar to show the options.

### Filter by Customer

1. Click in the **Add customer…** box.
2. Type to search for a customer.
3. Select one or more from the list.
4. They appear as chips below. Click the **×** on a chip to remove it.

### Filter by User

Choose from the dropdown:

- **None** — See bookings from everyone
- **Current user** — See only bookings you created

### Saving Your Filters

Click **Save** to remember your choices. Next time you open the dashboard, these filters will be applied automatically.

### What’s Active

When you have filters on, they appear as chips in a row. Customer chips are grey; the user filter is blue. Click **×** on any chip to remove that filter.

---

## Customize Columns

You can choose which columns to show and change their order.

1. Click **Columns**.
2. Tick or untick columns to show or hide them.
3. Drag column headers in the table to reorder.
4. Click **Reset Columns** to go back to the default setup.

More columns are available (e.g. Booked Date, Vendor, Price, Truck, CO Number) — enable them from the **Columns** picker.

---

## Search in the Table

Most column headers have a search box. Type to filter rows by that column. Click **Clear Filters** to clear all searches.

---

## Main Actions

### Assign

- **One booked booking**: Opens a quick form. Pick truck and driver, then assign.
- **Several booked bookings**: Takes you to Route Optimization to plan them together.
- **If a non-booked booking is selected**: You’ll see an error — only Booked bookings can be assigned.

### POD

Upload or manage proof of delivery. Select exactly one booking (it must not be in Booked status).

### Cancel

Cancel a selected booking. You can choose whether it’s chargeable and add remarks.

### Close

Close a booking that’s been delivered.

### Expand / Collapse

- Click **Expand/Collapse** to open or close all rows.
- Click the arrow on a row to show or hide its details.

---

## Refresh

Click **Refresh** to reload the data. It also refreshes automatically every 5 minutes.
