## Edit a Vendor Trip

The Edit Vendor Trip feature is designed to allow users to **update and maintain accurate information** about vendor bookings. Its main purpose is to ensure that any changes in trip details are properly recorded and reflected in the system.

### Step 1: Access the Vendor Trip

•	Go to the **Master Dashboard.**

•	Use the search function to locate the **specific vendor booking** you want to edit.

•	Click on the **Vendor Trip ID** to open the trip details.

![Vendor Trip ID](/img/vendor_edit_tripid.png)

### Step 2: Make the Required Changes

•	The **Edit Vendor trip** Form will automatically open.

•	Update the necessary fields such as:

o	**Truck Number**
o	**Driver Name**
o	**Agreed Price**
o	Any other relevant trip details

•	Ensure all changes are accurate before proceeding.

![Vendor Changes](/img/vendor_trip_change.png)

### Step 3: Save and Confirm

•	Click on the **SAVE button** to apply your changes.

![Vendor Save Button](/img/vendor_edit_save.png)

•	The system will **update the vendor trip**, and the modifications will be reflected immediately in the dashboard.

•	Double-check the **updated trip details** to confirm that your edits have been successfully applied.
