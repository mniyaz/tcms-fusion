# Using Drafts in Booking

**Drafts** let you save a booking form halfway through and come back to it later. Think of it like saving your progress in a form so you don’t lose your work if you get interrupted.

---

## 🎯 What Are Drafts?

A draft is a **saved copy** of the booking form. You can:

- Fill in some details now and finish later
- Keep up to 20 different drafts
- Load any draft, update it, and save again
- Delete drafts you no longer need

---

## 📍 Where to Find Draft Options

On the booking form, you’ll see these buttons at the bottom:

| Button | What it does |
|--------|--------------|
| **New Booking** | Clears the form and starts a fresh booking |
| **My Drafts** | Opens a list of your saved drafts |
| **Save Draft** | Saves the current form as a draft |
| **Update Draft** | Saves changes to the draft you loaded (appears when you load a draft) |

---

## 💾 Saving a Draft

1. Fill in the booking form (customer, addresses, goods, etc.).
2. Click **Save Draft**.
3. A message confirms it’s saved.
4. The draft appears in **My Drafts**.

You can save as many as 20 drafts. Each one is stored separately.

---

## 📂 Viewing and Loading Drafts

1. Click **My Drafts**.
2. A pop-up shows all your saved drafts with:
   - Customer name and job ID
   - Summary (pickups, deliveries, goods, truck info)
   - Date and time it was saved
3. Click the **arrow (→)** or the row to load a draft.
4. The form fills with that draft’s data.
5. The button changes to **Update Draft** so you know you’re editing that draft.

---

## ✏️ Updating a Draft

If you loaded a draft and changed something:

1. Make your changes in the form.
2. Click **Update Draft** (instead of Save Draft).
3. Changes are saved to that draft — no new draft is created.

---

## ➕ Saving a New Draft from the List

While the draft list is open:

1. Click **Save current as new draft** at the bottom.
2. A new draft is created from your current form data.
3. This works even if you had a draft loaded — you get a copy instead of overwriting it.

---

## 🆕 Starting a New Booking

To clear the form and start over:

1. Click **New Booking**.
2. If the form has data, you’ll see a confirmation: *“This will clear all current data. Unsaved changes will be lost. Continue?”*
3. Click to confirm.
4. The form resets and scrolls to the top, ready for a new booking.

---

## ⚠️ Important: Drafts Are Saved Only on Your Device

Drafts are stored **on your computer or device**, not on the server. That means:

- They **stay on the machine** where you saved them.
- You **won’t see them** on another computer or phone.
- You **may lose them** if you:
  - Clear your browser data or cookies
  - Use a different browser
  - Reinstall the browser
  - Use private or incognito mode (drafts may not persist)

**To keep a booking permanently**, use **Submit Booking** when you’re ready. That sends it to the system and it’s saved online.

---

## 📋 Quick Tips

- Use drafts when you’re interrupted or need to wait for more information.
- Submit the booking when everything is ready so it’s saved permanently.
- You can delete drafts you no longer need from the **My Drafts** list (trash icon).
- The **New Booking** button is only shown when you’re creating a new booking, not when editing an existing one.
