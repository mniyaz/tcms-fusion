# Create Truck Maintenance

The Truck Maintenance module is designed to **record, track, and manage all service and repair activities** for trucks. 

By creating a maintenance record, can ensure:

- **Accurate logging** of service dates, suppliers, and costs

- Easy tracking of **truck performance** and upkeep history

- Quick access to **edit or review** past maintenance records

This helps maintain fleet reliability, reduce downtime, and control expenses effectively.

### Step 1: Open the Truck Maintenance Form

-Navigate to the Truck Maintenance module

-Click on the **Create New** icon

-The Truck Maintenance form will open

### Step 2: Fill in Truck & Maintenance Details

-Verify the date or change it by clicking the date field and selecting from the calendar

-Select the **truck number** from the dropdown 
 
  The tonnage and chassis number will auto-fill

-Choose the code/category as **Lorry Maintenance** and select the supplier

-Enter the description and amount

-Select the credit terms and provide the invoice number

### Step 3: Submit & Manage Records

-Upload the required document

-Click **Submit** to save the record

-The entry will now appear in the Truck Maintenance table

**-To edit:**

Click the **ID** of the record
Press Edit, make changes, and Save
