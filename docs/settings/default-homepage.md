# Default Homepage (Landing Page)

You can choose which page opens first when you log in. This becomes your homepage so you can skip straight to your most-used screen.

---

## How to set your default homepage

1. Go to **Profile** (click your name or avatar in the top-right corner).

2. Find the **Default Landing Page** section (with the house icon).

3. Use the dropdown to pick the page you want to see first after login. Options include:
   - Planner (Booking Schedule)
   - Master Dashboard
   - New Booking
   - AiO Booking
   - Quick Book
   - Planning
   - Distribution Planning
   - Route Optimization
   - Rejected Deliveries
   - Latest Activity
   - DO Search
   - CO Search
   - Vendor RFQ
   - Summary Bookings
   - Invoice Dashboard
   - Inventory
   - Profile

4. Click **Save as Homepage**.

5. The next time you log in, you’ll be taken to that page instead of the default planner.

---

## Notes

- Your choice is saved in your preferences and tied to your account.
- If you don’t set a homepage, the app defaults to the Planner (Booking Schedule).
- You can change your homepage anytime by returning to Profile and picking a new option.
