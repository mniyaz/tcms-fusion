# Approve Invoice

This guide outlines the steps to approve an invoice from the **Generated** tab.

---

> ℹ️ **Note:** After enabling the invoice approval step, invoice documents cannot be uploaded without approval.

---

# Steps

1. Go to the **Generated** tab, select one or more invoices using the checkboxes, and click the **Approve** button.

2. After approving the invoice, you can now upload it and move it to the next step.

3. You can show or hide the **Approve** button for a user by configuring **Settings → Access Table → Approve Invoice**.
