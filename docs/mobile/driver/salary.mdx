# 📦 Salary – Easy Guide for Drivers

Welcome! This guide will help to view **Salary** step by step. Just follow along — it’s simple!

## 📅 Step 1: Choose Year and Month

- Tap the **Select Year** box to choose the year <span style={{color: 'red'}}>(Required)</span>
- Slide horizontally and tap the **Select Month** box to choose the month <span style={{color: 'red'}}>(Required)</span>

## 💰 Step 2: View Earnings and Deductions

- Review your **Earnings** breakdown (Basic Pay, Overtime, Commission, etc.)
- Review your **Deductions** breakdown (EPF, Socso, Advances, Unpaid Leave, etc.)
- Check your final take-home pay on the green **Net Salary** banner at the bottom
