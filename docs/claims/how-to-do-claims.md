## Claims – Help Guide

### 🎯 Purpose of Claims

The Claims module is designed to manage and track financial transactions related to company operations. It ensures transparency and accountability by recording **Top-ups, Cash Advances, Petty Cash, Spending Reports, and Expense Categories.**

This feature helps users answer the critical question: **“Where is the money now?”** by providing detailed reports and insights into cash flow.

### 💰 Top-up

Displays **balance amount** and top-up done for the current month.

To add a temporary petty cash entry:

1.Click Add Temporary Petty Cash.

2.Enter **From Account, Date, Credit Amount.**

3.Click **Save** → entry updates in the Top-up table.

### 💵 Cash Advance

After top-up, distribute cash to drivers/employees.

Search by **Trip ID** or manually enter: **Paid To, Category, Amount.**

Click **Submit** → entry updates in Cash Advance table.

**Features:**

Filter by Driver (shows top-up vs credited amount).

Customize columns via **Column** button.

Export data in **Excel/PDF** formats.

### 🧾 Petty Cash

Drivers submit **actual trip expenses** with receipts.

User records petty cash by entering: **Paid To, Truck, Category, Amount.**

Click **Submit** → entry updates in Petty Cash table.

**Features:**

View by Driver (shows cash given vs submitted amount).

Calculates whether driver owes company or company owes driver.

Customize columns and export to **Excel/PDF**.

### 📊 Top-up Vs Spending

Provides consolidated reports:

-Total Top-up

-Cash Given

-Submitted by Driver

-Balance Amount

**Key actions:**

**Click Where is the Money Now** → shows outstanding driver balances and company dues.

View Top-up transactions, Driver-wise spending, Category-wise spending.

Click **Cash Flow Report** → visual flow chart of all transactions.

Export to **Excel/PDF.**

**🏷 Categories**

Manage expense categories independently.

**Actions:**

Click Add **Category → create new expense category**.

**Edit or delete** categories as needed.

### ✅ Key Benefits

Transparent tracking of **cash flow.**

**Driver-wise** accountability.

Easy **customization** of views.

**Export-ready reports** for audits.

**Visual representation** of spending patterns.
