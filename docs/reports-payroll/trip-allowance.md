## Trip Allowance Help Guide

### Purpose

The Trip Allowance feature helps manage and record allowances provided to drivers for their trips. It ensures accurate tracking of payments, **simplifies payroll**, and confirms allowance inclusion in payslips.

### Quick Steps

1.	Go to Reports & Payroll → Payroll >> **Trip Allowance.**

2.	Enter Driver Name and select **FROM–TO** dates.

3.	Click **LOAD DATA** to view allowance records.

4.	If allowance was added during **POD upload**, it will appear automatically.

5.	Use **EDIT** to make changes, then click **SAVE**.

6.	Once a payslip is generated, the **PAYSLIP GENERATED** column shows a tick mark.

7.	Data can also be downloaded in **Excel and PDF formats**.

![Trip Allowance](/img/trip_allowance_form.png)

### Notes

•	Ensure correct driver and date range for **accurate data.**

•	Trip allowance can be **updated manually** or via POD upload.

•	Always save after editing to maintain **consistency.**
