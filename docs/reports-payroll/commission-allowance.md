## Commission Allowance

### Purpose

The Commission Allowance feature helps **manage and record allowances** provided to drivers for their trips. It ensures accurate tracking of payments, simplifies payroll, and confirms allowance inclusion in payslips.

### Quick Steps

1.Go to **Reports & Payroll → Payroll >> Commission Allowance.**

2.Enter **Driver Name** and select **FROM–TO** dates.

3.Click **LOAD DATA** to view allowance records.

4.If the **allowance percentage** was added during POD upload, it will appear automatically.

5.The invoice amount gets updated at the **SALES** column.

6.Use **EDIT** to make changes, then click **SAVE**.

7.Based on the **SALES and %** given, the trip commission will be auto-calculated.

8.Once a payslip is generated, the **PAYSLIP GENERATED** column shows a tick mark.

9.Data can also be downloaded in **Excel and PDF** formats.

### Notes

-Ensure **correct driver** and date range for accurate data.

-Always **save** after editing to maintain consistency.
