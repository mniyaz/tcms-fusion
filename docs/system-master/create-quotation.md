### üìç Navigation Path

Go to: System Master ‚Üí Customer ‚Üí **Quotation**

Click on the ‚ûï icon to create a **new quotation**. This will open the Create Quotation form.

## üîÑ Steps to Create a Quotation

### 1. Customer & Quotation Details

-Select the Customer Name and **Quotation Type** from the dropdown.

-Choose the **FROM and TO** locations along with the Tonnage.

-Click on the **Add Quotation Location** icon. 

-The selected location will appear in the **Location Price List**.

### 2. Drop Point & Waiting Charges

-In the Drop Point field, enter the applicable price or percentage.

-For Waiting Charges, input the **price or percentage** based on the number of hours.

## 3. Other Charges

-Select **additional charges** from the dropdown (e.g., Hand Jack, Trolley, etc.).

-Enter the price and click on the **Add Other Price** button. 

-The charge will be added to the Other Charges List table.

### ‚úÖ Final Step

-Click on the **SUBMIT** button. 

The quotation will be saved and reflected in the **Quotation List table**.
