# Create an Employee

### Purpose

The Create Employee feature allows **HR staff** to quickly add new employees into the system with all necessary personal, employment, payroll, and licensing details. It ensures that each employee has the correct **role-based access, payroll setup, and login credentials**, while also maintaining a secure record of required documents. This streamline onboarding and helps keep employee information centralized and accurate.

### Step 1: Open the Employee Form

Navigate to **System Master → HR Master.**

Click on the **Create New** icon to open the Create Employee form.

The **Employee ID** will be auto-generated.

![Create Employee Form](/img/create_employee_form.png)

### Step 2: Enter Employee Details

**Fill in the required information across these sections:**

**Personal & Contact Information:** First/Last Name, NRIC/Passport No., Date of Birth, Address, Work Phone, Mobile, Email ID. → Login credentials will be sent to the entered email.

**Employment Information:** Date of Joining, Department, Role (module access is based on role).

**Payroll Information:** Pay Group, Method, Pay Rate, SOCSO No., EPF No., Bank Account Details.

**Expiry Date Alerts:** Driving License No. & Expiry Date. For drivers: GDL No. & Expiry Date.
Settings Access: Enable View Settings checkbox if the employee requires access.

### Step 3: Upload Documents & Save

Upload soft copies of required documents **(IC, photo, DL).**

Add any additional documents if needed.

Review all details carefully.

Click **Submit** to finalize employee creation.

**✅ Quick Tip:** Double-check email and bank details before saving, as they are critical for login and payroll processing.
