# Create Supplier

### Purpose

The Create Supplier feature allows you to **add new suppliers** to the system. This ensures accurate records of supplier details, banking information, and contact persons, making **supplier management more efficient** and organized.

### Step 1: Access the Supplier Form

•	Navigate to **SYSTEM MASTER** → Vendors & Suppliers → **Suppliers**

•	Click on the **CREATE NEW** icon

•	The Create **New Supplier form** will open, and a **Supplier ID** will be auto generated

![Create Supplier Form](/img/create_supplier_form.png)

### Step 2: Enter Supplier Information

•	Fill in Business Details: **name, address, phone number, email**

•	Provide Registration Details: **SST, TIN (optional), company registration number**

•	Select **Credit Terms** from the dropdown

•	Enter Bank Details: **bank name, branch, account number, swift code, account holder name**

### Step 3: Add Contacts & Upload Profile

•	Enter Primary and Secondary contacts for Operations and Finance: **name, email, phone, mobile**

•	Secondary contact details are **optional**

•	Optionally, upload a **Profile Picture**

•	Review all details and **save** the supplier record

### ✅ Once saved, the supplier will be added to your system and available for future transactions.
