# How to Reset Customer/Vendor Portal Credentials

This guide explains the steps to reset the **User ID and Password** for both the Customer Portal and the Vendor Portal. The process is identical for both portals.

## Purpose

**Client Portal Reset:** Ensures customers can regain **secure access** to their portal in case they forget or lose their login credentials. This helps maintain uninterrupted access to services, account details, and communication.

**Vendor Portal Reset:** Allows vendors to quickly restore access to their portal when **login credentials** are misplaced or compromised. This ensures smooth business operations, timely updates, and secure collaboration with the organization.

### Steps to Reset Credentials

1.Navigate to the **System Master.**

2.Click on the Customer menu (for **Customer Portal**) or the Vendor menu (for **Vendor Portal**).

3.Select the **relevant Customer ID or Vendor ID** and choose the Edit option.

4.Click on the **Reset** button.

5.An email containing the **User ID and Password** will be sent to the respective customer or vendor.

![Customer Reset](/img/customer_reset.png)

### Alternative Method (Self-Service by Customer/Vendor)

Customers and vendors can also reset their credentials directly from their respective portals:

1.Go to the Customer Portal or Vendor Portal.

2.Click on **Forget Password.**

3.Provide the **Customer ID or Vendor ID.**

4.Click on the **SEND MAIL** button.

5.The respective person will receive an email with their new login credentials.
